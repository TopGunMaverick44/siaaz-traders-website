About Us

From Dubai to Pakistan: A Journey of Luxury

A Message from Our CEO

Welcome to H2O Home! For over 15 years, we have been dedicated to creating exceptional bathroom spaces that combine functionality with style. Our journey began in Pakistan, where we proudly operate four showrooms, offering world-class bathroom solutions to our valued clients.

As we expand into Dubai, we are excited to bring our expertise and passion for excellence to the UAE market. Our first showroom in Dubai marks the beginning of a new chapter, where we aim to provide the same exceptional service and high-quality products that have become our hallmark.

Thank you for trusting us as your partner in redefining bathroom luxury. Whether in Pakistan or the UAE, we look forward to helping you create beautiful and inspiring spaces that stand the test of time.

FOUNDER / CEO – H2OHOME

MOHAMMED IBRAHIM YOUNUS

Luxury Bathroom Boutique

Your Vision, Our Expertise

With over 12 years in Pakistans building material industry, H2O Home is committed to delivering contemporary European luxury bathrooms and exclusive interior products. Our goal is to transform everyday bathrooms into stunning spaces, showcasing the latest in design and style.
At H2O Home, we know that your bathroom is a reflection of your personal style. Thats why we offer a diverse range of premium products to help you design a space thats uniquely yours. Our skilled team of 3D designers collaborates with you to craft a custom bathroom that perfectly matches your needs and preferences. From luxury designer tiles and bespoke sanitary ware to elegant floor solutions, our collection is carefully selected to ensure top quality in every detail.
When you visit our showrooms, youll discover Pakistans finest array of designer bathroom tiles, fittings, and sanitary ware. With five outlets across Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad and a showroom in Dubai H2O Home is easily accessible across the region.
We believe every bathroom should be a retreat, and our luxury bathtubs and Jacuzzis offer the ideal space for relaxation. At H2O Home, were your ultimate destination for luxury bathroom solutions. Whether upgrading an existing space or designing a new one, our products and services are here to bring your dream bathroom to life.

A Hub for Luxury Bathrooms

Our showrooms in Pakistan are thoughtfully designed to showcase a wide range of high-quality bathroom fittings and finishes. These spaces allow our clients to see and experience our products in realistic settings, making it easier to envision their dream bathrooms.
